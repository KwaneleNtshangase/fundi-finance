⚡ Pro Tips for Getting the Most from Claude Design

1

Always reference your Design System first

Claude Design has your Fundi Finance Design System saved. Open a new design and type "Use the Fundi Finance design system" before adding your prompt — this locks in colours and typography automatically.

2

Specify dimensions explicitly

Always include the exact pixel dimensions (e.g. 1080×1080px, 1920×1080px). Claude Design uses this to set the correct canvas ratio. Without it, it defaults to a generic size.

3

Give aesthetic references, not just briefs

Saying "Inspired by Apple card dark mode" or "Bloomberg editorial style" unlocks a completely different output than a plain description. Claude Design reads aesthetic language.

4

Use the "Needs review" items in your Design System

Your Design System shows 15 items needing review. Go through each — approve the Brand Colors, fix the "Missing brand fonts" by uploading Geist from /public/fonts/ or Google Fonts CDN. This makes every future design sharper.

5

Iterate with "make it more ___" commands

After your first output: "Make it more confrontational" / "Increase contrast" / "Make the number much bigger" / "Add more breathing room" are the highest-yield iteration commands.

6

Export at 2× for retina displays

When exporting from Claude Design, always choose 2× resolution. This ensures your assets look sharp on modern phones and retina displays — critical for social media where images are compressed.

7

The missing Calculator & Budget Planner screens

Your Design System chat noted these two screens are missing. Add them with: "Design a mobile calculator screen for Fundi Finance showing a compound interest calculator with principal, rate, years inputs and a results graph in green. Use the Fundi Finance design system."
